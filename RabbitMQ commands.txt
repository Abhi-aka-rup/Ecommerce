RabbitMQ commands

1. rabbitmq-server.bat --> Starts the RabbitMQ broker
2. rabbitmqctl.bat start_app/stop_app --> To start/stop the RabbitMQ broker
3. rabbitmq-plugins enable rabbitmq_management --> To enable the RabbitMQ Management plugin, which provides a web-based interface for managing and monitoring RabbitMQ.

DEFAULT PORT: http://localhost:15672/
